Academic publishing is where science community pays publishers to publish and read the articles, and does editing for free.

In 2015, global academic publishing revenue was 30bil $. Music industry reported 15bil $. Publishers’ profits reach 30%.

Guardian: Is the staggeringly profitable business of scientific publishing bad for science?

We think this is not ok.

Dislib is the decentralised academic publisher.

Whitepaper

About

We are currently creating a network and foundation for distributed publishing.

Goal of the foundation will be to transition the full scientific community towards community-led publishing.

We propose to use the blockchain (Ethereum) and to create a currency to be used for publishing or downloading scientific articles. We will use the blockchain to keep the immutable records of who published what where.

We propose the peer-to-peer sharing methods (IPFS) as the backbone to keep the articles. You just have to connect existing libraries to it.
