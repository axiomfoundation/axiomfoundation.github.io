Decentralized Research Projects on Ethereum

Axiom transforms the current academic peer reviewing and publication system into a decentralized market and open access library for research projects.

Launch Axiom

Vision

Axiom aims to solve pressing issues of the current academic peer reviewing and publishing system

The current academic peer reviewing and publication system faces challenges, such as wrongly aligned incentive mechanisms, unnecessary intermediaries, and the existence of various biases. Axiom aims to solve these issues by creating an efficient, fair, and unbiased decentralized and market-based ecosystem for research projects to advance the scientific progress.

Challenge

Wrongly Aligned Incentive Mechanisms

Pseudo-altruism leading researchers to favor the provision of papers over equally important services to the academic community such as writing high-quality reviews and the publication of datasets.

Solution

Marketization

By creating a market for research projects, we aim to fix wrongly aligned incentive mechanims. We aim to achieve efficient resource allocation by making the value of services to the scientific community explicit (e.g. the price of a review).

Challenge

Unnecessary Intermediaries

Intermediaries such as publishers add little value but take the profit, e.g. by charging for publishing of and access to research assets. However, the creation of research assets is almost solely done by researchers. Learn More.

Solution

Disintermediation

By cutting out unnecessary intermediaries such as publishers, Axiom helps to unlock resources and to compensate researchers fairly.

Challenge

Existence of Various Biases

Biases such as gender, nationality and institutional affiliation affect the scientific progress. They are facilitated by a lack of transparency of the reviewing and publication process that is exploited by closed circles.

Solution

Full Transparency

Axiom helps to increase the transparency of the peer reviewing and publishing process. By doing so, it helps to mitigate current biases in the system.

In 2015, the global academic publishing revenue totalled USD 30 billion with a profit margin of around 30%.

Development

Axiom Technology Stack

Our current technology stack consists of IPFS, Ethereum, Solidity Smart Contracts, Metamask, React and Bootstrap.

Axiom Frontend

  • React for reference implementation of Axiom library
  • Twitter Bootstrap for landing page

Axiom Backend

  • IPFS for decentralized storing of immutable time stamped research assets
  • Solidity ERC 20 tokens as a means of value exchange of research assets and for marketization
  • Solidity smart contracts for review process management
  • Metamask for interacting with the Ethereum blockchain through a browser
